Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

By clicking on one of the crystals below you will add a specific amount of points to your score.

To win the game you must match your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts over the crystals will have new values.
